How to make Pancakes

Relevant Pages Include Page 1, Page 2, & Page 3

Items Needed

  1. A cooking pan
  2. Pam Cooking Spray
  3. Pancake mix of your choosing
  4. Any heating element like a stove
  5. Spatula

Step One

  1. Heat up the stove to medium to high heat
  2. Mix the pancake mix with water until it isn't too chunky or too runny

Step Two

  1. Once the stove is warm enough spray Pam onto the pan
  2. Pour the already mixed pancake mix onto the pan
  3. Wait roughly 2 minutes or until its a golden brown color

Step Three

  1. Use the spatula to take the pancake out of the pan and put it onto a plate to cool
  2. Enjoy your pancakes!
